Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Before sending a message, you might find the answer to your question in our FAQs. We've compiled responses to some of the most common inquiries to help you quickly find what you need!

Q: How do I find recipes for specific dietary needs (e.g., vegetarian, gluten-free)?

A: On our Quick Recipe Library page, you can use the "Quick Filters" (coming soon!) to sort recipes by dietary preferences. We're continuously adding more options to cater to diverse needs. In the meantime, many recipes offer substitution suggestions in their descriptions.

Q: Can I submit my own recipes to TastyCooksy?

A: We love hearing from our community! While we don't currently have a direct recipe submission portal, we encourage you to share your culinary creations on our Cooksy Community Wall. We regularly feature user-submitted ideas and might even turn your concept into a full TastyCooksy recipe!

Q: I'm having trouble with a recipe. Can you help?

A: Absolutely! The best way to get help with a specific recipe is to use the contact form above and mention the recipe name in your subject. Our team of cooking experts will do their best to guide you through any tricky steps or offer troubleshooting tips.

Q: How can I report a technical issue with the website?

A: If you encounter any bugs, broken links, or other technical problems, please use the contact form above and select "Technical Issue" as your subject. Provide as much detail as possible, including the page you were on and the steps to reproduce the issue. Your help ensures a smooth experience for everyone!

Q: Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

A: Currently, TastyCooksy focuses on providing online recipes and video guides. However, we are always exploring new ways to engage with our community! Stay tuned to our blog and social media channels for announcements about potential future workshops or live cooking sessions.
